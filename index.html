What is a YouTube Thumbnail?

A YouTube thumbnail is the preview image that represents a video. It’s the first thing people see when browsing videos, and it significantly affects click-through rates.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Paste the URL of a YouTube video into the input field.
  2. Click "Get Thumbnail".
  3. Click on the thumbnail to download it or right-click and choose "Save image as...".

Is It Legal to Download Thumbnails?

Yes, downloading YouTube thumbnails is generally legal if you use them for personal, non-commercial purposes. Always respect copyright and the creator’s rights when using thumbnails.
